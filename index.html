SPHEReD Lab

Software Engineering and Pedagogy in Higher Education Research and Development

About Us

The SPHEReD Lab, founded by Dr. Shaimaa Ali, is dedicated to advancing software engineering, data science, and pedagogy in higher education. We conduct interdisciplinary research focused on improving software engineering practices, data science applications, learning technologies, and evidence-based educational practices.

Research Focus

  • Software Fault Localization (Debugging)
  • Program Comprehension
  • Machine Learning Practical Applications
  • Effective Pedagogical Methods for Software Engineering
  • Educational Psychology for Software Engineering

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between software engineering research, data science, and teaching practices, and to empower higher education through innovative, research-driven software solutions and pedagogical frameworks.
